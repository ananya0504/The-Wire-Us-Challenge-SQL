Q3. The SQL FAST_FORWARD Cursor is one of the fastest cursors we have.
This SQL FAST_FORWARD Cursor is a combination of FORWARD_ONLY, and 
READ_ONLY. It means the FAST_FORWARD cursor will only move from the 
first row to last and does not support the scrolling backward.
